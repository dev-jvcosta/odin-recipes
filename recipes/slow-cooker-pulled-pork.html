Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Descrição

Slow Cooker Pulled Pork is the perfect combination of convenience and flavor. With a simple yet flavorful seasoning, the pork cooks slowly in the slow cooker, resulting in tender, juicy pieces that melt in your mouth.

Ideal for sandwiches, tacos, or served with rice and beans, this dish is an American classic that conquers palates around the world. Get ready for an explosion of flavor with minimal effort!

Ingredientes

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder (or butt)
  • 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce (optional)

Passos

Preparing the recipe:
  1. Season the pork: In a small bowl, combine the paprika, brown sugar, cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork.
  2. Prepare the slow cooker: Place the seasoned pork in the slow cooker. Pour the beef broth and apple cider vinegar over the pork.
  3. Cook on low: Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-5 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Shred the pork: Remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it with two forks.
  5. Mix with sauce: If desired, mix the barbecue sauce with the shredded pork.
  6. Serve: Serve the pulled pork on hamburger buns with your favorite toppings, such as coleslaw, pickles, and red onion.

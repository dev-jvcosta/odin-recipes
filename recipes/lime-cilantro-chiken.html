Slow Cooker Lime Cilantro Chicken

Descrição

Slow Cooker Lime Cilantro Chicken is an explosion of fresh and vibrant flavors, perfect for those looking for an easy and delicious meal. The combination of juicy chicken with the citrusy touch of lime and the herbaceous aroma of cilantro creates an irresistible dish that pleases all palates.

Prepared in the slow cooker, the chicken cooks slowly in a sauce based on salsa, taco seasoning, and lime juice, absorbing all the flavors and becoming incredibly tender. The result is a light, healthy, and flavorful dish, ideal to be served with rice, tortillas, salads, or as a filling for tacos and burritos. A practical and versatile option for everyday meals, which will surely become a classic in your kitchen.

Ingredientes

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 medium lime, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Passos

Preparing the recipe:
  1. Stir salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice, and cilantro together in a slow cooker until well combined.
  2. Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over top to coat.
  3. Cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, on Low for 6 to 8 hours or High for 4 hours.
  4. Shred chicken and serve.
